Accommodation, Stay at Work & Return to Work

Expert support to help employees and employers navigate workplace accommodations with personalized, evidence-based plans.

Get Started Talk to an Expert

What Is This Service

Expert Support for Workplace Accommodations

Using a functional model, we understand a person's abilities and limitations to collaborate with employees and employers on safe, effective plans for staying at work or returning after an illness or injury.

  • Comprehensive functional assessments
  • Personalized accommodation recommendations
  • Collaboration with healthcare professionals
  • Evidence-based return-to-work planning

Who Is This For

Services for Employers & Employees

Our programs support all parties in the accommodation process

For Employers & HR

Meet your Duty to Accommodate requirements with expert guidance and assessments.

  • Reduce workplace absence costs
  • Ensure legal compliance
  • Get expert accommodation recommendations
  • Access detailed functional reports

For Employees

Get the support you need to stay at work or return safely after illness or injury.

  • Understand your abilities & limitations
  • Communicate needs to your employer
  • Receive personalized support plans
  • Navigate the accommodation process
